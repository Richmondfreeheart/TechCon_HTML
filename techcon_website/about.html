Our History

TechCon began in 2010 as a local meetup for developers and has grown into a global event that brings together thousands of attendees each year. Over the past decade, it has evolved into a hub for innovation and collaboration in the tech industry.

Our Mission

TechCon is dedicated to fostering innovation, promoting inclusivity, and empowering future technology leaders. We aim to bridge the gap between emerging technology and the people who shape it.
